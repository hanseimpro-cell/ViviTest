In cooperation with the Törn e.V., Viviane Vanessa Eggers and a team of international women are organizing the first Impro-Spirit-Retreat in Colombia. More precisely, in the beautiful city of Barichara, a magical place in the middle of the mountains.

A very special experience awaits the participants from September, 14th to 24th, 2026.

What is the Impro-Spirit-Retreat?

Unleash your creativity – at the Impro-Spirit-Retreat!

Are you ready for a special kind of adventure? Immerse yourself in a unique 10-day improv theatre retreat that will not only develop your playful skills, but may also take you on a transformative journey.

Experience improv theater in a new way

In our Impro-Spirit-Retreat, you will get to know the art of improvisational theater in a more profound and playful way. Our experienced trainers (see below) will accompany you on an adventure that not only gives you creative freedom, but also helps you to deepen your spontaneity in a very special environment. Without everyday worries or festival stress, finally only the moment really counts. You and the Now.

Connecting with nature and spiritual experiences

But that’s not all: to unleash your creativity, we combine improvisational theatre with the healing power of nature and the fire of South American culture!

In the midst of a breathtaking landscape we offer you a variety of activities close to nature – be it a meditative hike that is going to touch all our four senses or a “Sound healing” course, breathing and physical exercises in the open air , for example drum yoga sessions in the morning, or time for reflection around the campfire. 

Cultural learning and community

We also immerse ourselves in local traditions and creative forms of expression, for example, with a traditional handcraft course or a Salsa Workshop. As the hosts cooperate with a small theatre in the city of Barichara, there is the possibility of a showcase and thus “giving something back” to the local community.

Impro classes / trainers

Tony Beeman

Tony is a veteran improviser, director, and educator based in Seattle, Washington, where he serves as the Artistic and Education Director of Unexpected Productions—the region’s oldest and largest improv company. Over a career spanning more than 25 years, Tony has performed, taught, and directed across Europe, the Middle East, and North America. His work is defined by a commitment to theatricality and sincerity, focusing on improvisation that feels like traditional theatre through ensemble storytelling, poetic language, and grounded authenticity.

As a director and creator, Tony has overseen more than 30 full-run productions, specializing in both organic long-form improv and improvised plays. His show  Four Hearts , an improvised homage to the works of Edward Albee, has been adapted by theater companies in Canada, Colombia, and Japan.

Felipe Ortiz

Felipe is a performer, director, and teacher interested in exchanging, interacting, playing, and sharing experiences with different artists worldwide. He is passionate about the body in movement and its possibilities; and explores it through acrobatics, improvisation, clowning, physical theater, and object manipulation to create new expression forms in performing arts. Since 2003, he’s been touring the world with different projects in festivals, companies, and artist collaborations. He is well-known in the international scene as an improv, physical theatre and clown teacher.

Viviane Vanessa

Viviane Vanessa has been an improviser since 2006 and a member of the “Steifen Brise” ensemble in Hamburg from 2011 until 2026, graduating with a diploma in acting in 2012 and has been on stage ever since.

In between, she worked as an actress with a permanent engagement at the Westfälisches Landestheater.

She is a trainer for improvisational theater, acting and role work and was also employed as a director for the first time in 2022. She teaches (+ performs) in Hamburg, as well as at national and international festivals and has been the first chairwoman of the “Törn-Verein”.

Specials

Vibrational Ritual

With this ritual, we will officially open our retreat. During the first hour, we will focus on moving and clarifying the intention each person brings, using somatic practices that help you connect with your highest frequency—your authentic self. This will be followed by an hour of sound vibration, allowing you to embark on this journey with the openness needed to embrace all parts of yourself—resistance, fear, judgment, and your creative power—and align fully with your intention.

This ritual will be facilitated by Patricia Macaya, an expert in sound healing and somatic practices.

IN-NAHA Ritual

In-Naha is a restorative somatic practice that works with the available energy of the field. Through this session, you will explore:

  • Releasing stagnant energy stored in your cellular memory
  • Reconnecting with sensations of joy, calm, trust, and gratitude
  • Regulating your nervous system through somatic resources
  • Opening your intuitive, receptive channel beyond the mental space
  • Processing bodily impulses that have been blocked by traumatic experiences

This exploration will support and enhance your skills as an improviser, an actor and performer of all kinds.
